Why do you want to be a web developer?

The web, at times, is so full of possibility and open to innovation, it often feel limitless. However, that infinite world has a structure. One that I find grounding. Web development is an opportunity to not only "restore a house" per se, but an opportunity to "build an entirely new house." With the skills of web development, I will be in a better position to finish my degree in computer engineering and perhaps become a member of a team that wants to "build homes AND their landscape." Whatever the future may carry in its rucksack, I know that understanding web development will help answer many questions I hold about the digital universe and lead to a satisfying career.
